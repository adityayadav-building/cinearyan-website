Common Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

Do you only work with multiplex chains, or also single screens?

Both. While PVR INOX is our largest client, a significant share of our work is with independent single-screen and boutique cinemas across India. Every hall gets the same design rigor regardless of size.

How long does a typical installation take?

It depends on hall size and scope, but a standard single-screen audio system install typically runs 5–10 days from equipment arrival to final calibration. Multi-screen projects are scoped individually.

Can you service equipment you didn't originally install?

Yes. We service Dolby, Crown, JBL, and most major cinema audio brands regardless of who installed them originally. We'll assess your existing system before quoting any work.

What's included in an AMC contract?

Comprehensive plans cover parts, labour, and emergency callouts. Non-comprehensive plans include scheduled preventive visits and labour, with parts billed separately. We'll recommend the right fit based on your equipment's age.

Do you provide pricing on the website?

Pricing depends on hall dimensions, equipment configuration, and service scope — so we quote per project rather than publishing fixed prices. Reach out with your requirements and we'll respond with a detailed quote.
