Dual 15" System

US-CA1532

Dual 381mm (15") Low Frequency System

A dedicated LF bin built around twin CA1860 15" drivers, ported between the two cones for high-output bass reinforcement. Where the US-CA3310 bass bin handles screen channel duty, the US-CA1532 stands alone — built for installations that need serious dual-15 low end without the M/HF horn section on top. A flexible building block for custom screen channel or LFE configurations.

Assembled in India
Components from USA
Request a Quote
What makes it different

Two CA1860 drivers in one enclosure share the load, dropping distortion at high SPL compared to a single 18" doing the same job. 101 dB sensitivity keeps amplifier demands reasonable even in large halls. EIA standard polarity. Enclosure dimensions: 450 × 884 × 762 mm (18" × 34.8" × 30").
